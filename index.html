Let’s Get Started

Time to get the most out of your upcoming celebration. Here’s a quick look into the steps of your event selection and journey with 2ND II NONE.

  • IT ALL BEGINS WITH YOUR INQUIRY - Submit your information form online to pass on your contact information, request services availability, and share some ideas about your upcoming event. Be specific about your event and let us know if you already have a team member in mind you’d like to work with. Our office team will follow up to connect with you about pricing, service options, staff accessibility and your additional questions. You can also call into our office directly to speak with a member of our team. We’re excited to hear from you.
  • SCHEDULE A MEETING - Now that you’ve qualified our services it’s time to meet an event host / concierge / photographer. Start telling your story, sharing your ideas and begin processing your project event goals and commence mapping out your event plan of action.  
  • RESERVE YOUR DATE- Let’s make it official. Guarantee your services, staff and equipment for your event date with your contract and deposit.
  • TIME TO SHARE - Your planning process with us begins. Time to log into your personalized online portal and event app to start sharing all the details of your event. Cultivate your music playlists, create your timelines, and expand our knowledge of your event experience through our informational planning forms.
  • REVIEW & FINALIZE - About a month before your event we schedule an in-person meeting to review all of your planning forms and event information. This final strategy session formulates and arranges your plans into your day of event schedule and guidelines.
  • TIME TO CELEBRATE - Your event is finally here so get out there and enjoy all of your hard work.
